Little introduction:

Hello I'm Andrew a.k.a A41K. I've been making websites and programming for over half a decade now. This was mostly a passion project that I've tinkered about making. First I did it with Framer then I thought "Maybe I should make something proper with my knowledge and the rest is now history. I think it's very important to say that this is a half-baked website with sub-par programming.

I specialize in creating responsive, user-friendly websites and applications. My goal is to combine technical expertise with creative design to build digital experiences that not only look great but also function flawlessly.
